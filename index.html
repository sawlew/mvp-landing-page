About

UnzipIt was born out of a shared frustration with the cumbersome process of dealing with compressed files. As students at Holberton School, we often found ourselves needing to extract ZIP files for various projects and assignments. However, existing solutions were either too complex or lacked the features we needed.


Determined to simplify this process, we embarked on a journey to create a user-friendly web app that would streamline file extraction. Over the course of several weeks, we collaborated closely, combining our expertise in web development to bring UnzipIt to life.


This project is not just a requirement for Holberton School; it's a testament to our passion for solving real-world problems through technology. We are proud to present UnzipIt as a Portfolio Project, showcasing our skills and dedication to innovation.
